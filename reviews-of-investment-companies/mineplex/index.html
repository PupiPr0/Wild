Conditions for investors

MinePlex is a mobile bank with a liquid token. The company combines a classic financial system, blockchain technology and modern services. The creators have developed a MinePlex wallet so that you can use cryptocurrency anywhere and anytime.

The way to earn money with the company comes down to staking. This is the receipt of passive income by storing and exchanging coins. At the heart of MinePlex's activities are two tokens (digital assets):

Mine. A unit of computing power of the blockchain. Its cost is 0.01 dollars. There is no volatility.
Plex. The token is issued once a minute. The price is $0.20.
There are few reviews about the project on the web. To find out more, we contacted the manager. To be fair, he was active and quick to respond to questions. It is clear that the company is interested in new customers.

To work, you must create your personal account on the MinePlex website. The procedure is standard and does not require special skills. The next step is to replenish the balance. To do this , use:

bitcoins;
electronic wallets.
The minimum amount is $100. Then you need to buy lanes. The basic package is $299. These assets are sent to a pool (shared boiler). There is a generation of plex tokens that are traded on several exchanges.

The more expensive the package, the more bonuses and privileges it gives. After a month, you can make a profit of 20% of the value of the initial investment. The whole point boils down to the following: buy mine tokens, get others (plex) for their storage. Exchange them profitably on the mine or fix your income in cryptocurrency.

You can make a profit the next day. You just need to bring plexes to the stock exchange, sell them and withdraw profits. MinePlex plans to issue a debit card. With its help, you can pay with cryptocurrency daily and in any country.

The earnings scheme is quite confusing. It will be difficult for a novice investor to understand the details of the process. For those who have never encountered these, it is better to bypass this company.

Network Marketing

You need to buy packages with mines through an affiliate network. When registering, the manager provides a unique ID code, without which identification is impossible. Then the user can send his code to referrals. When viewing packages, you can see percentages and bonuses for attracting a customer base.

MinePlex works on the principle of network marketing. Moreover, the payment for attracting partners is more than for storing assets.

Inconvenient replenishment of the account

You can deposit your funds only with cryptocurrency or through payment systems. Such schemes are used by dishonest firms, since transactions using such technologies are difficult to track.

First you need to create a personal account, confirm your e-mail and phone number. Such conditions are uncomfortable for potential investors.

MinePlex is not suitable for experiments with cryptocurrency. You can easily lose your deposit. In a new field for yourself, it's better to start small.
