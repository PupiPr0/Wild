    1. Design and User-Friendliness:

    • Tangem: Tangem devices feature a convenient card design, making them compact and easy to store and use.
    • Ledger: Ledger also provides compact hardware wallets, but Tangem stands out with its minimalist and intuitively designed card.

    2. Physical Access Security:

    • Tangem: Tangem utilizes special cards with a high level of security, making them resistant to physical attacks.
    • Ledger: Ledger also ensures a high level of security, but Tangem offers a unique physical form factor.

    3. Asset Management:

    • Tangem: Tangem provides a straightforward way to manage assets through its mobile app, making the process more intuitive.
    • Ledger: Ledger also offers software for asset management, but Tangem is often praised for its user-friendly approach.

    4. Comprehensive Integration with Mobile Devices:

    • Tangem: Tangem actively integrates with mobile devices through its mobile app, providing user convenience.
    • Ledger: Ledger also supports mobile apps, but Tangem is known for its effective integration.

    5. Cryptocurrency Support:

    • Tangem: Tangem supports various cryptocurrencies and tokens, making it a versatile storage solution.
    • Ledger: Ledger also provides a wide range of supported cryptocurrencies.

    6. Referral Link:
